Останні новини

A few years ago, frontend development seemed like a pretty straightforward field. You had to learn HTML, CSS, a little JavaScript, and understand responsive design — and you could create websites, landing pages, simple interfaces, and gradually move on. Today, many beginners feel that frontend has turned into a huge and confusing ecosystem. React, Vue, Angular, TypeScript, assemblers, routing, application state, SSR, SSG, API, testing, design systems, accessibility, optimization, CI/CD — the list goes on and on.

Повідомлення

Frontend has become more powerful, but at the same time more complex. Now it is no longer enough to know one technology - you need to know many at once.
